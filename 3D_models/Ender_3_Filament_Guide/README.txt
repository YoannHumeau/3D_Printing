# Summary

Update 7/24/2018: Someone asked for a matching right-side endcap so I've added one. 

I couldn't get another Ender filament guide to fit on my printer as it required pretty tight tolerances so I made one that just snaps onto the top of the 2020 extrusion. 


# Print Settings

Printer Brand: Creality
Printer: Ender 3
Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: .2mm
Infill: 10 - 100 %

# Installation

## Option 1

Slide this on from the end of the extrusion

![Alt text](https://cdn.thingiverse.com/assets/ed/d6/41/25/04/snapon1.jpg)

## Option 2

Snap it on from the top about halfway, then slide it on the rest of the way. 

![Alt text](https://cdn.thingiverse.com/assets/6d/f9/57/9e/4b/snapon2.jpg)

![Alt text](https://cdn.thingiverse.com/assets/6c/6a/2e/01/8b/snapon3.jpg)

## Note

You won't be able to snap it on from the top in its final position unless I add a slice where the clip and arm meet to allow the clips to flex. This might weaken the part or make it looser than I wanted it to be so I didn't do it.
